<p>Openreads is the best app for keeping track of your books that is free, open source, with no ads and no tracking!</p>
<p>It is a reading list app that will help you organize your library with three lists provided:</p><ul>
   <li>books you finished</li>
   <li>books you are currently reading,</li>
   <li>books you want to read later.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can add the books by searching them up in Open Library, scanning their barcode or by entering the book's details manually.</p>
<p>You can also see come cool statistics!</p>
